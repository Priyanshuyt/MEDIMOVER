Advanced Features

Sanitization Chamber

Built-in UV disinfection system that eliminates harmful pathogens during item delivery, ensuring safety in healthcare environments.

Line Following

Advanced sensors allow Medimover to accurately follow predefined paths throughout hospital floors with precision navigation.

Remote Control

Manual navigation options through intuitive mobile app and remote control for situations requiring human intervention.

Crash Detection

Sophisticated sensors alert and automatically stop Medimover upon impact, preventing accidents and ensuring safety.

AI Obstacle Recognition

AI-powered camera system detects and identifies people and objects in real-time, enabling intelligent navigation decisions.

Secure Compartments

Access-controlled storage compartments ensure that only authorized healthcare staff can retrieve sensitive medical items.

How Medimover Works

Loading and Assignment

Medical staff load items into Medimover's secure, sanitized compartments and assign a delivery destination through the intuitive interface or mobile app.

Navigation and Delivery

Using its advanced line-following technology and AI-powered obstacle detection, Medimover navigates safely through hospital corridors to reach its destination.

Secure Handoff

Upon arrival, only authorized personnel can access the secure compartments using their unique credentials, ensuring chain of custody for sensitive items.

Return and Redeployment

After successful delivery, Medimover either returns to its charging station or proceeds to the next assignment, maximizing efficiency in hospital logistics.
